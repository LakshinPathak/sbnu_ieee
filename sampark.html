SAMPARK'24

SAMPARK is an annual IEEE Gujarat Section event organized by IEEE Gujarat Section for students where all the student members showcase IEEE activities carried out by their respective student branches and plan the activities for the coming year. SAMPARK has always been one of the most important events of IEEE Gujarat Section, as it brings together all the IEEE student branches under one umbrella for better perspective and innovative dimension. The event also provides a platform to share and discuss their ideas and views with Section Executive Committee and fellow student members.

Perks of IEEE

  2. IEEE provides a wide range of quality publications that make the exchange of technical knowledge and information possible among technology professionals.
  3. IEEE sponsors over 2,000 annual conferences and events worldwide, curating cutting-edge content for all of the technical fields of interest within IEEE.
